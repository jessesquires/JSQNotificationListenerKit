NotificationObserver

public final class NotificationObserver <V, S: AnyObject>

An instance of NotificationObserver is responsible for observing notifications.

Warning

When an observer is initialized, it will immediately begin listening for its specified notification by registering with the specified notification center. To stop listening, dealloc the observer by setting it to nil.
